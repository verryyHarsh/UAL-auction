Winning Criteria

  • 1
    Team Rating

    The team with the highest total player rating wins the auction.

  • 2
    Tie-Breaker

    If two or more teams have the same total rating, the winner will be decided based on the highest remaining purse (budget).

  • 3
    Team Size

    Each team must build a squad of exactly 11 players within the specified budget.
